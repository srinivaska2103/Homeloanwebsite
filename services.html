Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to your most pressing concerns.

Which home loan is right for me?
The right home loan depends on your income, credit score, property type, and repayment capacity. Our advisors assess these factors during a free consultation to recommend the most suitable product.
What documents are required?
Standard documents include KYC (ID/Address proof), income proof (salary slips/ITR), bank statements, and property documents. Requirements vary slightly based on employment type.
How long does loan approval take?
With complete documentation, pre-approvals can happen within 48-72 hours. Full disbursement typically takes 7-14 working days depending on the bank and property legal checks.
Can I transfer my existing loan?
Yes, a Balance Transfer allows you to move your loan to another lender offering lower interest rates, which can significantly reduce your EMI or tenure.
What is the minimum credit score?
Most banks prefer a CIBIL score of 750 or above for the best rates. However, loans are available for lower scores (650+) with slightly varied terms.
Do you help with documentation?
Absolutely. We provide end-to-end documentation support, ensuring your file is perfectly prepped for rapid bank approval.
Is online consultation available?
Yes, we offer fully remote consultations via phone or video call, and digital document collection for your convenience.
Are there any hidden charges?
We maintain 100% transparency. Any processing fees levied by the bank will be clearly communicated upfront.
